Beschreibung:

My set of M+ Helpers - Explosive Orbs hide all Nameplates (except orbs) on Pressing specific modifier key (alt/shift etc.) - Hero/Bloodlust reminder (when in bossfight and of cd) - for Affixes Like Bolstering by glowing the highest totalhp unit (Places a marker/glowlow on the highest health mob of the pack and keeps moving it while you damage them. [DOES NOT OVERWRITE EXISTING MARKERS]) - for things like raging by glowing the lowest totalhp unit (Places a marker/glow on the lowest health mob of the pack and keeps moving it while you damage them. [DOES NOT OVERWRITE EXISTING MARKERS]) You can disable the marking in Custom options. if you do not want the weakaura to glow go to actions -> on show and untick the "glow external element" checkbox Sanguine Helper Inspired by https://wago.io/ByV3k3OLG Explosive Orbs hide nameplates minor edited version of https://wago.io/4N7vVthan all credits to Causese Every feedback/help is greatly appreciated feel free to contact me by joining my .
